Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We're here to serve you fresh coffee throughout the day!

Do you offer free WiFi?

Yes! We provide free high-speed WiFi for all our customers. Whether you're working, studying, or browsing, stay connected while enjoying your coffee.

Can I customize my coffee order?

Absolutely! We're happy to customize your drink with different milk options (oat, almond, soy), sweetness levels, extra shots, and more. Just let our baristas know your preferences!

Do you have vegan or dairy-free options?

Yes! We offer oat milk, almond milk, and soy milk as dairy-free alternatives. We also have vegan pastries and snacks available.

Do you accept reservations for groups?

We welcome groups! For parties of 8 or more, we recommend calling ahead at +63 912 345 6789 so we can prepare a comfortable space for your group.

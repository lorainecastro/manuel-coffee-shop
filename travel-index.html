Premier Tourist Trails of the Philippines

An exquisite selection of the nation’s most distinguished hiking destinations — offering serene beauty, geological wonder, and profound natural splendor for discerning adventurers.

#1

Mount Pulag

Benguet · 2,922 m · The Sea of Clouds

Renowned for its ethereal dawn sea of clouds above rolling grasslands — a transcendent experience on one of the country’s most accessible high peaks.

#2

Mount Apo

Davao Region · 2,954 m · Highest Summit

The Philippines’ supreme mountain, revered for its pristine montane forests, crater lakes, and majestic multi-day ascent.

#3

Masungi Georeserve

Rizal · Limestone Karst Formations

An elegant fusion of conservation and design — suspended trails, sky bridges, and dramatic limestone peaks near Metro Manila.

#4

Mount Batulao

Batangas · 811 m · Open Ridges

Graceful rolling ridges and panoramic vistas — an ideal introduction to Philippine mountaineering.

#5

Mount Pinatubo

Tarlac/Zambales · Volcanic Crater Lake

A journey to a serene turquoise lake cradled within an ancient crater — a testament to nature’s resilience and beauty.

#6

Mount Daraitan

Rizal · Emerald River & Caves

Challenging path concluding at a luminous river and hidden limestone caverns of rare beauty.

#7

Tarak Ridge

Bataan · Knife-Edge Ridge

Dramatic exposed ridge offering sweeping views across Manila Bay — for the intrepid hiker.

#8

Mount Ulap

Benguet · Pine Forests & Views

Gentle trails through cool pine groves and strawberry fields — serene Cordillera scenery.

#9

Mount Pico de Loro

Cavite/Batangas · Parrot’s Beak

Iconic monolith viewpoint atop a beloved Tagaytay ridge trail.

#10

Mount Makiling

Laguna · 1,090 m · Rainforest Biodiversity

Lush montane forest rich in endemic flora, culminating in sweeping southern vistas.

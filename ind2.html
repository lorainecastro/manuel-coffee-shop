Top 10 Tourist Trails in the Philippines

Discover the most breathtaking hiking destinations across the archipelago — from beginner-friendly paths to challenging summits that reward with unforgettable views.

#1

Mount Pulag

Benguet · 2,922 m · Sea of Clouds

Iconic grassland summit famous for the magical sea of clouds at dawn. Beginner to intermediate friendly with multiple trails.

#2

Mount Apo

Davao · 2,954 m · Highest Peak

Philippines' tallest mountain. Multi-day trek through mossy forest, lakes, and stunning crater views.

#3

Masungi Georeserve

Rizal · Limestone trails

Unique rope courses, hanging bridges, and dramatic karst formations. Perfect day trip from Manila.

#4

Mount Batulao

Batangas · 811 m · Rolling ridges

Scenic beginner-friendly hike with open views, mini-summits, and campsites.

#5

Mount Pinatubo

Tarlac/Zambales · Crater Lake

4×4 ride + moderate hike to a surreal turquoise crater lake inside an active volcano.

#6

Mount Daraitan

Tanay, Rizal · Tinipak River

Challenging trail ending with emerald river and limestone caves. Very photogenic.

#7

Tarak Ridge

Mariveles, Bataan · Knife-edge

Famous for its dramatic ridge walk and panoramic views of Manila Bay.

#8

Mount Ulap

Itogon, Benguet · Easy summit

Gentle trail with pine forests, strawberry farms, and great Benguet views.

#9

Mount Pico de Loro

Cavite/Batangas · Parrot's Beak

Popular hike with the iconic rock formation "Parrot's Beak" viewpoint.

#10

Mount Makiling

Laguna · 1,090 m · Forest trail

Biodiverse rainforest trail near Los Baños with waterfalls and panoramic Laguna views.
